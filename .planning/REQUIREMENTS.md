# Requirements — bac_ses

## REQ-001: Topic Browser
Display SES syllabus topics organized by theme (Science Économique, Sociologie, Regards Croisés). Each topic shows its sub-topics and a quick status indicator (not started / in progress / mastered).

## REQ-002: Flashcard System
Per-topic flashcards with a question on the front and answer on the back. Tap to flip. Mark confidence (again / good / easy) — spaced repetition by next review date.

## REQ-003: Quiz Mode
Multiple-choice and short-answer quizzes generated from the flashcard pool. Score tracking per session. Wrong answers bubble up for review.

## REQ-004: Progress Dashboard
A simple overview: topics mastered, cards due for review, quiz streak, estimated readiness score. Persisted in localStorage.

## REQ-005: Key Concepts Glossary
Searchable glossary of SES terminology (definitions, examples, related concepts). Browsable by theme.

## REQ-006: Exam Simulation
Timed quiz mode that mimics the BAC exam format — dissertation-style prompts and/or MCQ sections with a countdown timer.
